Orange FM ROM v1.2 Changelog

- New: Limited support for pre-release firmware 0.4
	- Custom theme support not implemented
	- LED setting disabled
	- Otherwise all other features/fixes should be working
- New: Nicer error if MCU never responds to the boot sequence
- New: Show ROM and FW versions during bootup
- Bugfix: Booting the cart with auto-amp on, and headphones inserted, could result in a burst of audio through the system speakers. This has been fixed.
- Bugfix: Fix a graphical glitch that could occur when changing frequency range or spacing
- Bugfix: Changing the frequency spacing no longer seeks if it's not needed
